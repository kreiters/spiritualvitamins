April 2024 - I AM

Exodus 3:14 I AM who I AM. Then Moses said to God, when they ask me who YOU are, what shall I say? And the Lord said to Moses, I AM.

April 2024 letter on the I AM statements

Who is God? Who is Jesus? I went to the Bible to answer these questions. In the quote from Exodus 3:14 God is declaring that He is not a new god but The God of creation. He is eternal, unchanging, self-existing and THE GOD of all. We will see that Jesus also claims the same declaration in the I AM series. They are one and equal in the Trinity. The verse above and the I AM verses of Jesus validate the claim of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, being three in one.

I began my search using the Jeremiah Study Bible. In addition to the scripture Dr. Jeremiah's charts and footnotes were most helpful. I also found an internet source titled, “Voice of Prophesy”, and a contributor named Jean Boomstra. Her comments were very insightful. My thanks to both for their much needed contribution. There are seven I AM's in the book of John.

Day 1. John 6:35 “I AM the Bread of Life. He who comes to Me will never hunger. ” In Jesus’ day bread was considered to be the staple food source in a household. Here Jesus is saying He is the staple spiritual source for you and me. Is Jesus your basic spiritual staple?

Day 2. John 8:12 “I AM The Light of the World.” On day one of creation God said, “Let there be light. He saw that the light was good, so He separated the light from the darkness.” Light helps us see the correct way to travel through life. Do you walk in the good light or do you stumble in darkness?

Day 3. John 10:9-11 “I AM the Door.” What is the function of a door? Closed doors provides protection from danger. An open door beckons friendship and admits entry to those we love. Is your door open to Jesus' teaching and love?

Day 4. John 10:11-14 “I AM the Good Shepherd.” We are like sheep and need a Shepherd. They follow the flock as do we. Both need protection. The Good Shepherd's sheep hear His calming voice. Do you obey His calm voice?

Day 5. John 11:25 “I AM the Resurrection and the Life.” Life is our most valued gift. Jesus gave us the gift of His life on the cross. His gift is Life saving!Have you unwrapped His gift for you yet ? His gift was death and resurrection.

Day 6. John 14:6 “I AM the Way, the Truth and the Life.” You will never get lost if you follow His way, and you can believe everything Jesus says because He is always truthful. The Bible, however, reports Satan, as the Father of Lies!

Day 7. John 15:5 “I AM the True Vine.” Jesus said He is the true vine, Father God is the Caretaker, and believers are the branches. God prunes the branches to produce good fruit. God wants believers to produce much fruit.

Day 8. John 8:58 “ Before Abraham was born I AM” This was a confounding statement to the Pharisees. Jesus was claiming to be GOD YAHWEH. The Jews refused to see Christ's Glory so they looked for rocks and tried to stone Jesus. Do you understand God's majesty and give Him the glory due Him?
