April 2023 - The Holiness of the Lord

"HOLY, HOLY, HOLY. And one (angel) called out to another and said, 'Holy, Holy, Holy, is the LORD of hosts, the whole earth is full of His glory." Isaiah 6:3

In 1969 at age 31 While living in Tucson, Arizona I accepted Jesus as my savior. My family and I joined a small church near our home named Evangelical Covet Church. It was small but welcomed us with warm hospitality.

A small choir of about 8-10 people were loyal and were robed for duty every Sunday. When Tim and I joined the choir it expanded to a full dozen. The morning service always opened with the Hymn, "The LORD is in His Holy Temple" as we processed down the center aisle and the congregation prepared their hearts to silently join in the second stanza, "Let all the earth keep silence before Him" thereby showing their love and respect for the worship of the LORD.

Those memories have remained with me for over 50 years and caused me to seek to share some of my ideas on God's Holiness this month.

The definition of Holy is Sanctified, dedicated or Sacred. From Exodus in the Old Testament to the books of Romans and 1 Peter in the New Testament God has chosen to enlighten us on His Holiness and to tell us what else is Holy, namely:

1. The Trinity is Holy. Father, Son and Holy Spirit. (read Acts 3:14) From Genesis to Exodus and in Moses' Song in Habakkuk 1:12 the Lord gives us proof.

2. God's Name is Holy. (read Psalm 145:21 and Luke 1:49)

3. The Sabbath Day is Holy. (see Genesis 2:3 and Exodus 20:8)

4. The Name of the Lord is Holy. (read 2 Timothy 1:9 and 2 Timothy 3:16)

5. Believers who are devoted to God and are dedicated to the work of God are called Saints. (read Romans 12:1)

What did I learn about our Holy God from these verses? God is sacred and worthy of our love and worship because of His Holiness! All He has created is provided in His holiness for believers. Faith in the "Holy One" is our key to living a holy life. Holiness is much to be desired!

Psalm 11:4 The Lord is in His holy temple, the Lord's throne is in heaven.
