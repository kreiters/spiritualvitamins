June 2024 - Hope

Proverbs 23:17-18 "Do not let your heart envy sinners; but be zealous for the fear of the LORD all day long; for surely there is a hereafter, and your Hope will not be cut off."

HOPE VITAMIN LETTER

Hope is an interesting word. It is a short four letter word with a huge potential for life now and for the future. However, Christian hope is a gift from Jesus and His redemption. He is the reason for hope and is the one who restores hope.

Worldly hope is based on wishful thinking and it is only available until our physical life ends. Christian hope has its foundation in the person of Jesus who died for our sins and gave us a promise for the future of eternal life in heaven.

Scripture has a lot to say about hope. It is defined as comfort, expectation, confidence and trust. We are to direct our life in these areas if we seek all that God has planned for us. Psalms has good instructions on the subject of our forever hope.

"My soul, wait silently for God alone; for my expectation is from Him." Psalm 62:5. "We delight our Lord when we fear and love Him, who hope in His mercy." Psalm 147:11. "" You are my hope O Lord God; You are my trust from my youth." Psalm 71:5.

The man, JOB, is one of my most admired people in the Bible. In spite of illness, pain, death of all his children and loss of all his wealth, Job was totally committed to the Lord. Here are his words in Job 13:15. "Though HE (God) slay me, I will hope in HIM." And again in Job 27:8, "For what is the hope of the godless when he is cut off, when God requires his life?" The answer is, no hope! If we do not choose to hope in Christ in this earthly life, where the result of that decision is eternal life in heaven, then we have no choice after death. The very worst circumstances for you and me is to loose all hope. This leads to spiritual death and eternity in Hell.

May God bless you as you seek to live in the hope and love of the Almighty in this life and the next. I always find good research in the Jeremiah Bible and The Bible Knowledge Commentary. Once again I have used both for my comments and I say, Thanks, once again.
