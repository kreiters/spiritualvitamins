August 2024 - GOD'S MESSAGE ABOUT HATE

Matthew 6:24 "No one can be a slave to two masters; he will hate one and love the other; he will be loyal to one and despise the other."

Lately on television I have noticed a commercial by Franklin Graham in which he says that he feels as if the whole world is aflame with hate. It seems to me that he is correct.

The hope of Christian life for eternity rests on the truthful words of God's loving promise. Romans 6:23 "Sin pays its wage in death; but God's free gift is eternal life in union with Christ Jesus our Lord.

I looked up Billy Graham's biography on my computer and I would like to share some of his wisdom on hate that I found. Here are six good ideas we can use when we are on the brink of hate.

1. People hate when they should love. Proverbs 17:17 2. Quarrel when they should be friendly. John 15:15 3. Fight when they should be peaceful. Romans 12:18 4. Wound when they should heal. Matthew 13:14 5. Steal when they should share. Galatians 6:6 6. Do wrong when they should do right. Proverbs 21:2

Does it seem to you that individually we all struggle with these observations? And as a nation each of the 6 ideas are compounded? Billy Graham gave us the world's observations but now I'd like to share the Biblical view from God's perspective. Proverbs 6:16-19 "There are six things which the Lord hates, yes seven which are an abomination (of his soul) to Him."

1. "God hates haughty eyes. "(pride) 2. "A lying tongue." (lies) 3. "And hands that shed innocent blood." (murder) 4. "A heart that devises wicked plans." (evil, trouble) 5. "Feet that run rapidly to evil." (mischief, evil folly ) 6. '"A false witness who utters lies. "(false word of untruth) 7. "And one who spreads strife among brothers."(gossip or slander)

What is the Answer? The answer is love. The answer is Jesus and His surpassing great love and goodness. 1 Corinthians 6:17 says, "But the one who joins himself to the LORD is one spirit with Him. Micah 6:8 "He has told you, O man, what is good; and what does the LORD require of you but to do justice, to love kindness, and to walk humbly with your God. "Best of all, remember the Golden Rule. "Whatever you want others to do for you, do so for them!"
