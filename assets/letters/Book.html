February 2023 - The Bible: God’s Book of Love Letters

“And now these three remain: FAITH, HOPE AND LOVE. But the greatest of these is LOVE.” (1 Corinthians 13:13)

(My prayer) “Lord, fill my mind with Your law and scripture. Fill my heart with Your love and obedience.”

I love February and Valentine’s Day as well as Easter and Christmas. These holidays all have God’s love in common! I use Valentine’s Day to observe the Biblical Law “Love your neighbor as yourself” and the Law to “Obey all God’s commandments.” Easter presents God Incarnate. A love so great He died on the cross for Believers in Him. Christmas reveals God’s love in a gift named Immanuel (God with us). Jesus performed miracles and created teaching situations which revealed His love for mankind and His Precepts and Commandments.

This February I’ve chosen Psalm 19:7-10 and Psalm 119:160 as Vitamins to teach us how to be ALMIGHTY LOVING. Quoting C.S. Lewis: “Though our feelings come and go, (God’s) love for us does not.”

Day 1: The Law (scripture, statutes) is Perfect (flawless) Restoring (replacing, re-establishing) the Soul (life spirit or spiritual element).

Day 2: The Testimony is Sure (secure and true) and Wise (prudent).

Day 3: The Precepts (commandments) are Right (correct direction) Rejoicing:(gladness) the Heart (seat of emotion).

Day 4: The Commandments (Instructional) are Pure (genuine, undefiled) and Enlightening (illumine).

Day 5 The Fear of the Lord (awe, dread, reverence) is Clean (wash, purified) enduring Forever (eternal).

Day 6: Judgment (condemnation) is True (real, reliable) Righteous (virtuous).

Day 7: Sum (total) God’s Word (message, speech) is Truth (genuine, honest).

Day 8: Lord’s Righteous (virtuous) Ordinances (statutes) Everlasting (eternal).

SCRIPTURAL EVIDENCE:

Day 1: Law is Perfect. (James 1:21)

Day 2: Testimony is Sure. Wise. (1 Corinthians 1:7,8, Psalm 111:7, James 3:13)

Day 3: Precepts Right. (Matthew 15:7,8,9, Rejoicing: Luke 13:17, the Heart.)

Day 4: Commandments (John 13:34) are Pure (1 Timothy 1:5), Enlightening the eyes. (Ephesians 1:18)

Day 5: Fear of the Lord (Psalm 15:11), Clean (John 15:3), Forever (Psalm 9:11)

Day 6: Judgments of the Lord. (Revelation 19:1,2)

Day 7: Sum of Thy Word (Matthew 4:4) is Truth. (Proverbs 23:23)

Day 8: Lord’s Righteous Ordinances (Exodus 12:14) Everlasting. (Psalm 90:2)
