The Seven Churches Overcome letter - May 2024

Revelation 1:5-6 "To Him who loved us and washed us from our sins in His own blood, and has made us Kings and Priests to His God and Father."

Revelation was written by the disciple John, who was a prisoner on the Isle of Patmos, to the 7 churches located in Asia. The 7 Spirits before the throne is the Holy Spirit. The mystery of the 7 stars seen by John is revealed as the 7 Angels of the 7 churches who hold the lampstands. The lampstands are not the light, but hold the light of God and His instructions to pass on to future believers. John then writes down the names of the 7 churches who hold the light, and the messages for us.

My thanks for the information, footnotes and charts used from the Jeremiah Bible, as well as help and enlighten from the Bible Knowledge Commentary by Dr. John F. Walvoord and Dr. Roy B. Zuck. "He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches."

Each church has 4 areas of Praise plus a Promise from Christ Jesus.

1. COMMENDATION, Christ knows their work. (except Laodicea)

2. A REBUKE, I have this against you, (except Smyrma and Philadelphia)

3. EXHORTATION, Repent, be overcomers.

4. AN ENCOURAGING PROMISE (each church receives a different promise)

A. The Church at Ephesus: I know your deeds and your toils and perseverance. But I have this against you. You have left your first love. Christ's Overcomer Promise:Eat from the tree of life. Revelation 2:7

B. The Church at Smyrma: I know your tribulation and poverty. Satan will test you. Overcomer Promise: Not experience second death. Revelation 2:11

C. The Church at Pergamos: You hold fast My Name and do not deny My faith. But I have a few things against you. Teach of Balaam, a blunder to the Jews, eat food sacrificed to idols, acts of immorality. Overcomer Promise: Eat of the hidden manna. Revelation 2:17

D. The Church at Thyatira: Your deeds are greater than first. But I have this against you.Tolerated Jezebel, immorality. Overcomer Promise: Have power over the nations. Revelation 2:26.

E. The Church at Sardis: I know your incomplete deeds. A few people are unsoiled. Overcomer Promise: Clothed in white garments. Revelation 3:5

F. The Church at Philadelphia: I know your deeds. I give you an open door. You have a little power. You have kept My Word and My Name. Revelation 3:8

G. Overcomer Promise: A Pillar in the Temple of God. Revelation 3:12 The Church at Laodicea: I know your deeds. You are Lukewarm so I will spit you out of My mouth. Those I love I discipline. Repent. Overcomer Promise: . Sit with Me on My Throne. Revelation 3:21

H. I Am the Alpha and Omega. He who overcomes will inherit all things.
