This month’s topic is the Sword of the Spirit which the Bible identifies as the Word of God. The Sword of the Spirit is one component of our defense against evil and Satan’s attacks. This defense is called The Armor of God. It is our protection, shielding, and safety as a Christian.

We have some wonderful examples in Scripture of God leading men who faced opposition as they served the LORD in their ordinary lives just as we are called to do. David, who became a King and an ancestor of Jesus, was just a teen-aged youth when he volunteered to fight the Philistine soldier named Goliath. According to 1 Samuel 17:4-7 this warrior was 9 or 10 feet tall and protected from head to toe with armor. David said,“You come with a sword, a spear, and a javelin, but I come to you in the name of the Lord of Hosts, the GOD of the armies of Israel”. David was victorious with 5 smooth stones. Goliath had man’s armor but David had God’s armor.

We can also look to the Apostle Paul as an example of a man like us who served the Lord clad in God’s armor. Romans 13:12-14 tells us “The light is almost gone, and the day is at hand, let us lay aside the deeds of darkness and put on the armor of light.” And verse 14 says “Put on the Lord Jesus Christ and make no provision for the flesh in regard to its lusts.”

The Word of God tells us:

1. Our HEAD must be protected by the HELMET of SALVATION. 2. Our HEART dwells in the BREASTPLATE of RIGHTEOUSNESS. 3. Our WAIST supports our EQUIPMENT with the BELT OF TRUTH. 4. Our FEET are CUSHIONED with SANDALS walking in PEACE and PROCLAIMING CHRIST’S GOOD NEWS. 5. Our HAND holds the SHIELD and allows us to advance in victorious FAITH. 6. Our SWORD of the SPIRIT is the only aggressive weapon as it uses GOD’S WORD, the BIBLE, to defeat the Devil!

ARE YOU PUTTING ON GOD’S ARMOR TODAY AND THIS WEEK?
