Christ in you --- The Hope of Glory

COLOSSIANS 1:27 “To them GOD willed to make known what are the riches of the glory of this mystery among the Gentiles which is Christ in you, the Hope of Glory. Also read Ephesians 3:3-6

The word hope is an interesting word. We just celebrated both Christmas and the New Year’s arrival. In modern America during those holidays many people were filled with hope that they would receive a certain gift or hoped that in this new year of 2022 we would have peace and prosperity. Their hope was just a wish with no assurance that it would happen. In our Colossians verse God has given us the biblical definition of His HOPE which has 4 primary biblical applications. God’s Hope includes comfort, trust, confidence and expectation. In the above verse Paul brings all four definitions to the believer. Christ in believers brings the HOPE (comfort, trust, confidence and expectation) of GLORY(Divine reward) to all who put their faith and trust in Christ Jesus, God’s only Son.

In Colossians 2: 13-14 Paul again tells Gentiles that God, having forgiven us of all our trespasses, having wiped out the handwriting requirements that was against us, which was contrary to us, reveals the mystery of eternal life now and in heaven. He (GOD) has taken the unknowns about our future out of the way. God nailed sin to the cross, which is the man JESUS. The word “handwriting” is the same word as our word for autograph. It was used as the signature on an IOU that was given in courts of justice to release slaves to their masters.

GOD erased our sin with the work of Christ on the cross. What a comfort that we can trust Jesus with our forever future. Believers have confidence that our eternity has been paid for by a loving God and our home will be in heaven. Can you say that you have this same expectation and are as confident as believers are told to be? You can have this same confidence by believing in His love for you and His payment for your sins by His sacrificial death on the Cross. Thank Him for His death for your sin and invite Him to be King of your life. Follow your decision by participating in water baptism which is a public announcement that you are committed to following Jesus.

1 Timothy 6:17 says, “Don’t fix your hope on the uncertainty of riches, but on GOD.The reason for this is found in Hebrews 6:17-20. “God is the anchor for our soul. Before Christ’s birth, death, and resurrection, the veil of sin separated us from God. Christ tore down the veil and now we have the anchor of forgiveness.

May you begin 2022 with CHRIST IN YOU, THE HOPE OF GLORY
